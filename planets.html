🧠 The Science of Weight vs Mass

Your mass never changes across the universe — it's the amount of matter in your body. Your weight is mass × gravitational acceleration. On Jupiter (gravity = 2.53× Earth), you'd weigh 2.53× more. On the Moon (0.166× Earth), you'd feel weightless and bouncy. Gravity: the original body-shaming since the Big Bang.
